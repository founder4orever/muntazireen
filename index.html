The Prophet ﷺ described the signs of the Hour in precise detail fourteen centuries ago. We document them here — sourced, referenced, and plainly explained.

Time Passes Faster
The Prophet ﷺ foretold that time would compress — a year feeling like a month, a month like a week. This is not metaphor. It is widely felt and increasingly documented.
Sahih Al-Bukhari 1036 — Grade: Sahih
02
Active Now
Earthquakes Multiply
Earthquakes would increase in frequency before the Hour. Global seismic data shows a marked increase in significant earthquakes over the last two decades.
Sahih Al-Bukhari 1036 — Grade: Sahih
03
Active Now
Barefoot Shepherds in Tall Buildings
Those who were poor and barefoot would compete in constructing tall buildings. The Gulf states — historically Bedouin — now host the world's tallest structures.
Sahih Muslim 8a — Grade: Sahih
04
Active Now
Knowledge Decreases, Ignorance Spreads
Scholars would diminish and ignorance would prevail. Misinformation, false scholarship, and religious illiteracy are at historic highs globally and within the ummah.
Sahih Al-Bukhari 80 — Grade: Sahih
05
Fulfilled
The Splitting of the Moon
This occurred in the time of the Prophet ﷺ as a miracle. The Quran opens Surah Al-Qamar with it. The Hour began drawing near from that moment.
Surah Al-Qamar 54:1 — Sahih Al-Bukhari 3636

The greatest trial humanity will face. One-eyed. Claims divinity. Crosses the earth except Mecca and Medina. The first 10 ayat of Al-Kahf are protection against him.
