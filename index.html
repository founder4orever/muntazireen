Time Compresses
The year feels like a month, the month like a week, the week like a day. This is not metaphor — it is a documented sign reported across the ummah globally.
"The Hour will not come until time passes quickly, so that a year is like a month, a month like a week..." — Tirmidhi 2332, Hasan

"The moon split during the time of the Prophet ﷺ into two halves." — Sahih Al-Bukhari 3636, Sahih
