Muntazireen — The Signs

What was foretold
is unfolding.

The Prophet ﷺ described the signs of the Hour in precise detail fourteen centuries ago. We document them here — sourced, graded, and plainly explained. No speculation. No sensationalism. The signs speak for themselves.

«اقْتَرَبَتِ السَّاعَةُ وَانشَقَّ الْقَمَرُ»
"The Hour has drawn near and the moon has split."
Surah Al-Qamar 54:1 — The Hour began drawing near from this moment.
Active Now — Minor Signs
Occurring in our time
01
Active Now
Time Passes Faster
The Prophet ﷺ foretold that time would compress — a year feeling like a month, a month like a week, a week like a day. This is not metaphor. It is felt globally and increasingly studied.
«لَا تَقُومُ السَّاعَةُ حَتَّى يَتَقَارَبَ الزَّمَانُ، فَتَكُونَ السَّنَةُ كَالشَّهْرِ»
"The Hour will not come until time draws close together, so that a year is like a month."
Sahih Al-Bukhari 1036 — Grade: Sahih
02
Active Now
Earthquakes Multiply
Significant earthquakes would increase before the Hour. Global seismic data from USGS shows a marked increase in M5.0+ events over the past two decades, consistent with the prophetic description.
«لَا تَقُومُ السَّاعَةُ حَتَّى... تَكْثُرَ الزَّلَازِلُ»
"The Hour will not come until earthquakes increase."
Sahih Al-Bukhari 1036 — Grade: Sahih
03
Active Now
Barefoot Shepherds in Tall Buildings
Those who were barefoot and destitute shepherds would compete in constructing tall buildings. The Gulf states — historically Bedouin — now host the world's tallest structures. Dubai, Riyadh, Doha.
«أَنْ تَرَى الْحُفَاةَ الْعُرَاةَ الْعَالَةَ رِعَاءَ الشَّاءِ يَتَطَاوَلُونَ فِي الْبُنْيَانِ»
"That you see the barefoot, naked, destitute shepherds competing in constructing tall buildings."
Sahih Muslim 8a — Grade: Sahih (Hadith of Jibril)
04
Active Now
Knowledge Decreases, Ignorance Spreads
Scholars diminish, and ignorance prevails. Knowledge is removed not by erasure, but by the death of scholars. Religious illiteracy and misinformation are at historic highs within and outside the ummah.
«إِنَّ اللَّهَ لاَ يَقْبِضُ الْعِلْمَ انْتِزَاعًا... وَلَكِنْ يَقْبِضُ الْعِلْمَ بِقَبْضِ الْعُلَمَاءِ»
"Allah does not take away knowledge by snatching it from people, but He takes it away by taking away the scholars."
Sahih Al-Bukhari 100 — Grade: Sahih
05
Active Now
Widespread Tribulation (Fitna)
Trials and tribulations would spread widely — in beliefs, in social fabric, in family structure. The Prophet ﷺ described a time when a man would be a believer in the morning and a disbeliever by evening.
«بَادِرُوا بِالأَعْمَالِ فِتَنًا كَقِطَعِ اللَّيْلِ الْمُظْلِمِ»
"Hasten to do good deeds before trials come like pieces of a dark night."
Sahih Muslim 118 — Grade: Sahih
06
Active Now
Widespread Killing
Killing would become widespread and confusion would surround it — the killer not knowing why he kills, the killed not knowing why he was killed. This describes multiple active theatres of war today.
«لَا تَقُومُ السَّاعَةُ حَتَّى يَكْثُرَ الْهَرْجُ. قَالُوا وَمَا الْهَرْجُ قَالَ الْقَتْلُ»
"The Hour will not come until harj (killing) increases." They asked, "What is harj?" He said, "Killing."
Sahih Al-Bukhari 7061 — Grade: Sahih
Fulfilled — Already Occurred
In the time of the Prophet ﷺ or thereafter
07
Fulfilled
The Splitting of the Moon
This occurred in the time of the Prophet ﷺ as a visible miracle witnessed by the companions. The Quran opens Surah Al-Qamar with its affirmation. The Hour began drawing near from this moment.
«اقْتَرَبَتِ السَّاعَةُ وَانشَقَّ الْقَمَرُ»
"The Hour has drawn near and the moon has split."
Surah Al-Qamar 54:1 · Sahih Al-Bukhari 3636 — Grade: Sahih
08
Fulfilled
The Arabian Peninsula Becomes Green
The Prophet ﷺ foretold that the Arabian Peninsula, then largely barren, would return to being rivers and meadows. Satellite imagery and geological studies confirm the peninsula was once fertile, and current greening programs align with this.
«لَا تَقُومُ السَّاعَةُ حَتَّى تَعُودَ أَرْضُ الْعَرَبِ مُرُوجًا وَأَنْهَارًا»
"The Hour will not come until the land of the Arabs becomes meadows and rivers again."
Sahih Muslim 157 — Grade: Sahih
09
Fulfilled
The Slave Woman Gives Birth to Her Master
Scholars interpret this as children becoming masters of their parents — the inversion of the family structure where children command and parents serve. Others reference the literal end of institutional slavery. Both have occurred.
«أَنْ تَلِدَ الْأَمَةُ رَبَّتَهَا»
"That the slave woman gives birth to her master."
Sahih Muslim 8a — Grade: Sahih (Hadith of Jibril)
Major Signs — The Ten
None have occurred yet
M01
Major — Awaited
The Emergence of Dajjal
The greatest trial humanity will face. One-eyed — his right eye like a protruding grape. Claims divinity. Crosses the earth in 40 days. Mecca and Medina are sealed against him. The first 10 ayat of Al-Kahf are protection.
«مَا بَيْنَ خَلْقِ آدَمَ إِلَى قِيَامِ السَّاعَةِ خَلْقٌ أَكْبَرُ مِنَ الدَّجَّالِ»
"There is no creation from Adam to the Hour greater in trial than the Dajjal."
Sahih Muslim 2946 — Grade: Sahih
M02
Major — Awaited
The Descent of Isa ibn Maryam ﷺ
Jesus ﷺ will descend near the white minaret east of Damascus. He will kill the Dajjal. He will break the cross, kill the pig, abolish the jizya. He will rule by the Shariah of Muhammad ﷺ.
«وَالَّذِي نَفْسِي بِيَدِهِ لَيُوشِكَنَّ أَنْ يَنْزِلَ فِيكُمُ ابْنُ مَرْيَمَ حَكَمًا عَدْلاً»
"By the One in whose Hand is my soul, the son of Mary will soon descend among you as a just ruler."
Sahih Al-Bukhari 3448 — Grade: Sahih
M03
Major — Awaited
The Emergence of Ya'juj and Ma'juj
Gog and Magog will breach the barrier built by Dhul-Qarnayn. They will spread through the earth and drink the seas dry. They will be destroyed by worms sent by Allah after the dua of Isa ﷺ.
«وَيَفْتَحُ اللَّهُ يَأْجُوجَ وَمَأْجُوجَ وَهُمْ مِنْ كُلِّ حَدَبٍ يَنْسِلُونَ»
"And Allah will open up Gog and Magog and they will rush down from every slope."
Sahih Muslim 2937 — Grade: Sahih · Al-Anbiya 21:96
M04
Major — Awaited
The Three Great Sinkholes (Khusuf)
Three great sinkholes: one in the East, one in the West, and one in the Arabian Peninsula — swallowing armies and populations. Scholars differ on interpretation. The signs are clear — the timing is with Allah.
«خَسْفٌ بِالْمَشْرِقِ، وَخَسْفٌ بِالْمَغْرِبِ، وَخَسْفٌ فِي جَزِيرَةِ الْعَرَبِ»
"A sinkhole in the East, a sinkhole in the West, and a sinkhole in the Arabian Peninsula."
Sahih Muslim 2901 — Grade: Sahih
M05
Major — Awaited
The Rising of the Sun from the West
The sun will rise from the west. When this occurs, the door of repentance is closed. Faith at that point benefits no soul that did not believe before. This is irreversible — prepare before it comes.
«لَا تَقُومُ السَّاعَةُ حَتَّى تَطْلُعَ الشَّمْسُ مِنْ مَغْرِبِهَا»
"The Hour will not come until the sun rises from the west."
Sahih Al-Bukhari 4635 — Grade: Sahih
M06
Major — Awaited
The Beast of the Earth (Dabbat al-Ard)
A creature will emerge from the earth and speak to people, marking the believers on their faces and branding the disbelievers. It carries the Staff of Musa and the Ring of Sulayman ﷺ.
«وَإِذَا وَقَعَ الْقَوْلُ عَلَيْهِمْ أَخْرَجْنَا لَهُمْ دَابَّةً مِّنَ الْأَرْضِ تُكَلِّمُهُمْ»
"And when the word is fulfilled against them, We will bring forth for them a creature from the earth speaking to them."
Al-Naml 27:82 · Sahih Muslim 2901

Methodology — How We Document Signs
Every sign on this page is drawn from Sahih (authentic) or Hasan (good) narrations only. Da'if (weak) hadith are not used without explicit labelling. All hadith are cited with their source collection and scholarly grade. Interpretation follows mainstream Sunni scholarship — we do not speculate beyond what scholars have established. If you find an error in grading or sourcing, contact us and we will correct it. We follow scholarship. We do not replace it.
