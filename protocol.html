The 12-Week Schedule

Structured. Progressive.
No guesswork.

Phase One
Weeks 1–2 — Anchor
Deen: All 5 prayers locked. Morning adhkar established. Al-Kahf Ayat 1–3 memorised.
Body: Push/pull/core 3x week. Brace before every lift. Knee-safe progressions.
Blade: Hammer grip × 20 draws daily. All 5 5 angles of attack introduced slowly.
Survival: Write your Tier 1 list. Order what's missing. Know what you own.
Phase Two
Weeks 3–4 — Lock In
Deen: Al-Kahf Ayat 1–10 fully memorised by end of Week 4. Adhkar unbroken.
Body: Intensity increases. Post-sprint drills introduced — stress inoculation begins.
Blade: All 5 angles at 60% speed, clean. Folder deployment introduced.
Survival: KI tablets and hand crank radio sourced. Tier 1 nearly complete.
Phase Three
Weeks 5–8 — Build
Deen: Aqeedah study begins — Kitab at-Tawheed. Understanding deepens beyond habit.
Body: Heavy sessions. Bag runs introduced. Numbers tracked and improving.
Blade: All 5 angles at full speed. Axe and Rambo fundamentals introduced.
Survival: Sawyer filter practised. Tier 2 food supply building. Maps and compass sourced.
Phase Four
Weeks 9–12 — Integration
Deen: Al-Kahf recited every Friday. Dua is personal and specific. Connected to community.
Body: Peak conditioning. Can carry a loaded bag for 60 minutes. Documented proof of growth.
Blade: Full pressure drill — all blades, post-sprint. Angle training with a partner — live pressure reps.
Survival: Full kit audit. Tier 2 nearly complete. Cat plan updated. First aid course booked.

Governing Principles

What this is built on.
What it will never compromise.

01 —
Paper-First
Everything is printable. Every schedule, guide, and gear list works without a phone. When the grid goes down, your knowledge doesn't. This is not a contingency — it is the design.
02 —
Referenced & Verified
Every hadith is graded. Every claim follows scholarship. Nothing here is speculation presented as fact. Sahih is cited as Sahih. Hasan as Hasan. We follow the scholars — we don't replace them.
03 —
Certainty, Not Fear
The Hour is Allah's promise fulfilled — not a threat. The believer who knows their Lord prepares with calm certainty. We do not panic. We prepare. There is a difference.
04 —
Free. Always.
No paywall. No premium tier. No merchandise. No email required. Sadaqah jariyah is the only model. If this helps one believer stand firm when the trials come, that is enough.
